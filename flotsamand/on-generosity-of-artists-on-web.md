A wallpaper created for my Maverick Meerkat desk. This is slightly tweaked from [LostAndTaken.com](http://lostandtaken.com/blog/2010/4/10/25-subtle-and-light-grunge-textures.html) which is the creation of the elegant, prolific, and generous [Caleb Kimbrough](http://lostandtaken.com/about/). I became aware of LostAndTaken via the blog [BitBox](http://www.bittbox.com/) which has all kinds of resources for digital art production.

There really isn't much to say other than to thank the kind artists of the world for sharing their work and Al Gore for [inventing the Internet](http://www.snopes.com/quotes/internet.asp).
